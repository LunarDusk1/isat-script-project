Odile Sus Events

Dialogue compiled by memeseller (tumblr baketothefuture)

Odile convo in the final room, if you act suspicious enough.

To unlock, read the Time Craft book in the Candle-making room (WIP) as well as the books on Wish Craft in the locked Storage Room (WIP) on Floor 1 and the Secret Library on Floor 2.

Additionally, the player needs to obtain a total of 14 Weird points and talk to Odile during the third Snack Break.

This event does not occur on loops with all Friendquests completed.

For more details on Weird points, check out the respective wiki page on the event.

Source: Common Event 132
