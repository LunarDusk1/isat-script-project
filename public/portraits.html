Game File to Wiki Portrait names

The game files and the wiki use different names for dialogue portraits, which leads to confusion when trying to look up specific portraits I use in this script. So, here's this!


There's a variety of reasons the wiki portraits are named as they are, and I'm personally responsible for like, 90% of them. Teehee?

Portrait names like "s_shit_1" are funny, but we're trying to look professional, okay.
