THE ISAT SCRIPT

The reason for this website's creation. I shall attempt to make a full script of hit indie game In Stars and Time, which everybody should play immediately.

All images used are taken from game files and hosted on The fantabulous ISATwiki!!!!

Index

  • Events (Events that only occur once/under special circumstances, e.g. Ghosts event)
  • Story (Events specifically tied to story progression)

Site News

RSS Feed changed from .txt to .xml

Date: May 05. 2024

All issues with the RSS Feed are now fixed! However, in the course of this, I've discovered I was apparently using the wrong format. As such, the RSS Feed is now correctly a .xml file. The remainder of the link is the same, so simply replace .txt with .xml in the url in your feed reader of choice.

New Formatting!

Date: May 05. 2024

Thanks to goldstarglowww, we have some new features! New pages now have the ability to toggle expressions on and off, in case you don't want to see what portraits are being used.

In addition to that, text formatting itself has gotten noticably fancier, with borders, and opacity, and all that. Do check it out. Since dialogue is back to VCR OSD Mono, the "ISAT Font" button has been changed to a Cascadia Mono button, which was the default font prior to this. Default font size now also matches the standard size of the VCR OSD font.

Applying these changes to all pages will take forever, though. So only new pages (and also Meadow) currently support this.

This has also somehow caused the sidebar to vanish on mobile. We are looking into it.


Do you like the change? Do you think it sucks? Do you have specific issues with layout, font sizing, or anything else? Please drop a comment on tumblr!
