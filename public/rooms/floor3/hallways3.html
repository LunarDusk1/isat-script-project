Left side Hallways


Smiling Key Door

Source: Map071 (Hallway (Locked 3-1))

The hallway immediately to the left of the central room. Leads to the pottery room (page WIP), the hallway leading to the Observatory, and the hallway with three rooms.

Without the key

Interaction text is generic. The key is the Smiling Key ("shaped like a smiling mask"), found in the room with the mirror on the right.

With the key

Interaction text is generic.


Frozen Housemaiden

Source: Map 048 (Hallway (Tears))

The hallway once-removed from the left of the central room; the top leads to Mirabelle's dorm, the Changing Room, and the Star Room, and the bottom has a row of tears and leads to the hallway to the bathroom.


Hallway to the bathroom

Source: Map105 (Hallway (Locked 3-3L))

The hallway below the one with three rooms; has a friend of Mirabelle's, and a locked door that leads to the hallway to the bathroom.

Right side hallways


Angry Key Door

Source: Map092 (Hallway (Locked 3-2))

The hallway immediately to the right of the central room. Leads the to the room with the crest, which is locked, and the hallway to the Mirror Room and Writing Room.

Without the key

Interaction text is generic. The key is the Angry Key ("shaped like a angry mask"), found in the Changing Room ("in one of the dorm rooms") on the left.

With the key

Interaction text is generic.
