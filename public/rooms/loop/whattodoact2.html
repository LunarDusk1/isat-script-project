Conversation structure

Uh. How the standard convo goes. PreDialogue happens first.


Loop (teehee4): Heya, stardust! How can I help you on this wonderful new loop?

Siffrin (questions1): (Questions, questions...) "What should I do next?"

Loop (hm1): What you should do? Well...


Insert What To Do (pictured below) here.


(...)


Loop (happy1): Anything else?

Siffrin: "Nah, nothing."

Loop (teehee5): See you later, stardust!


(They wave at you excitedly.)

First and second time:

(You awkwardly wave back as you go...)

Third to fifth time:

(You wave back as you go.)

Sixth time onwards:

(You wave back as you go, smiling.)


Start

If you go back to talk to Loop right after your first meeting with them.


Loop (hm1): Well, stars, I don't know.

Loop (teehee3): Did you get to the top of the House so you can fight the King yet?


(. . .)


Loop (hm1): I dunno! Just an idea!

Loop (teehee5): Come on stardust! You can do it! I believe in you~!

Siffrin (um2): (Urgh.) "You're sooooo helpful."

Loop (lol5): I really am. Thank you for noticing.

Loop (happy1): Don't worry. I know there's that big pesky trap in the way, but I'm sure you'll find a way around it, if you just look.


First Tear

After passing the Death Corridor successfully and got frozen by the first Tear.

Loop (happy1): Oh, you passed the trap, see?

Loop (lol5): Knew you could do it, stardust!

Loop (teehee4): And you found out about Tears, didn't you? Very useful little things. Annoying, too.

Loop (hm1): But you might find yourself in a situation where you need to loop back, and dying against a monster takes soooo long!


(They're not wrong...)


Loop (lol4): Of course I'm not wrong, stardust.

First Boss

If you reached the first boss but didn't beat it.

Loop (hm1): You're up against that big Sadness, aren't you?

Loop (lol4): It's not easy to beat, so make sure you're all stocked up on items before you go!

Loop (hm1): And, oh, don't forget you can loop back, well, forwards?

Loop (teehee1): If you found a way to die here, in Dormont, you'd be able to loop back to Floor 1.

Loop (teehee3): With all the doors opened, too, if you remember to press [button] when you're dead!

Siffrin (n-nya2): (But...) "How does that even work?"

Loop (happy2): You pretty much loop back and dissociate the whole way to wherever you need to go.

Loop (lol5): Your allies won't notice a thing though, so it's all good!

Loop (teehee3): Good luck with that big Sadness! Remember, if battles are tough, there's a few things you could do.

Loop (hm1): You can go and pick up tonics in the rooms you've seen, you can fight Sadnesses for EXP, make sure you and your party have the equipment you need...

Loop (teehee5): You can go to the Change God's statue in Dormont, too! Or help out some of the villagers, they might have a gift for you at the end!

Loop (lol5): Good luck, stardust! I believe in you!


(...)


Second Floor

Reaching the second floor before entering Euphrasie's office.


Loop (teehee1): You beat that big Sadness!

Loop (hm1): So you're on... Floor 2 now, right?

Loop (happy1): Some spooky things there. Nothing that dangerous, though!

Loop (lol1): The library's on that floor, too... You don't have to read the books there, but they're interesting!

Loop (teehee5): You're not quite halfway there yet, but you're close! Don't give up, stardust! I believe in you!


Rock Trap The Second

After the rock trap blocks off Euphrasie's office from reentry for the first time.


Loop (lol3): Big rock trap again, huh? That's what happens when you don't look hard enough, stardust!

Siffrin (hide1): (Urgh.) "Hard to see things with one eye."

Loop (happy1): Yeah, I know.

Loop (happy1): You'll miss things no matter what, especially with your sight reduced. You shouldn't feel bad.

Loop (teehee3): You can loop back and look everywhere again, after all!!!


(That was... a surprisingly nice thing to say.)


Loop (lol5): I'm always nice, stardust!


At the Favor Tree

Additional dialogue if you're talking to Loop at the Favor Tree instead of calling them.


Siffrin (shit1): (And yes, you came to see Loop, but...) "It's gonna be a pain to go back to Floor 2..."

Loop (hm1): Not really? You could always loop back, stardust!

Loop (happy2): So, you know, find an easy way to die somewhere in Dormont.

Loop (lol1): There's an easy loop-back spot somewhere. I wonder if you can find it...?


Library

After reaching the library but before the second boss.


Loop (happy3): You found the key in the Head Housemaiden's office! Congrats, stardust!

Loop (lol1): That library is quite something, isn't it? You definitely don't have to, but I'd recommend looking around and reading a bit!

Loop (teehee2): Knowledge is never wasted!

Seeing the second boss but not beating it.


Loop (teehee2): You found all the keys for the second floor, didn't you? Congrats, stardust!

Loop (oh my1): Big enemy ahead, though... Two whole enemies!

Loop (teehee3): Good luck with it! Remember, if battles are tough, there's a few things you could do.

Loop (hm1): You can go and pick up tonics in the rooms you've seen, you can fight Sadnesses for EXP, make sure you and your party have the equipment you need...

Loop (teehee5): You can go to the Change God's statue in Dormont, too! Or help out some of the villagers, they might have a gift for you at the end!

Third Floor

Reaching Floor 3 but before having to go back to Floor 2.


Loop (teehee2): You did it, you're on Floor 3!

Loop (happy3): This is the last floor before the King. This floor will have a bunch of Tears blocking your way, too.

Loop (teehee3): One of those blocked paths will surely have what you're looking for, but it doesn't mean the other paths are useless! Do your best!


Crying Key

After being forced to loop back due to missing the Crying Key from Floor 2.


Loop (hm1): Ah, you have to go back to Floor 2, don't you?

Loop (lol5): There's something waiting for you in the classroom on Floor 2! How exciting!

Siffrin (hide1): (...) "I missed something again."

Loop (hmmm1): Stardust, you're missing an eye. Of course you'd miss things.

Loop (lol4): Maybe you wouldn't miss things if your party took the time to help you search instead of chatting.

Siffrin (serious1): (Hey...) "It's not their fault."

Loop (well2): Sure, it's not.

Loop (happy1): And it's not your fault either, stardust.


End of Floor 3

After unlocking the last door with the Crying Key but before you see the third boss.


Loop (lol4): Ah, you're so close to the end of Floor 3, stardust!!! So close!!!

Loop (hm1): Too bad you have to backtrack for that key...

Loop (hmmm1): Who knew the key for a door on Floor 3 would be in the classroom on Floor 2?

Loop (teehee4): Have fun walking there, stardust~!

Loop (lol3): Remember! The library is on the second floor!
